Probabilistic Connection Importance Inference and Lossless Compression of Deep Neural Networks

Xin Xing, Long Sha, Pengyu Hong, Zuofeng Shang, Jun S. Liu

Abstract: Deep neural networks (DNNs) can be huge in size, requiring a considerable a mount of energy and computational resources to operate, which limits their applications in numerous scenarios. It is thus of interest to compress DNNs while maintaining their performance levels. We here propose a probabilistic importance inference approach for pruning DNNs.
